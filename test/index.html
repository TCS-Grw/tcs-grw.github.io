Introduction Of The Blogging Team!

Introduction Of The Blogging Team!

Hello and Assalamualaikum. Welcome one, welcome all. It’s a pleasure to meet you on this lovely day as the Director of The City School blog. Here a team of enthusiastic participants is joining hands to enlighten your mind with our sparkling content. We ensure to accord you with informative yet captivating content but let me introduce myself first. My name is Zainab Ifrahim, a student just like you because I believe that a person, no matter the age, never stops learning and growing. A little bit about myself, I’m a curious person seeking opportunities to try new things. I have been a part of the student council, participated in sport events representing my school and even took part in a

National Debate Competition – giving studies and physical activity equal importance.

Bonjour! I hope everyone’s well I’m Ariba Pirzada from 11T and I’m stoked to be given this opportunity to share little snippets of my life with you guys! I love making my friends laugh, am completely obsessed with Lana del Rey and a huge potter head!! I could go on about Harry Potter for hours! The Anatomy fascinates me and I’m so intrigued by it that I’ve done some extra courses about it too! I also have a sweet tooth and could have sweets for breakfast, lunch and dinner! Though I want a career in medicine I believe literature and writing are one of life’s greatest pleasures and I do hope you guys enjoy what my friends and I write!

Hi, my name is Muhammad Salman and I am from Class 11-T. I’ve been granted this extraordinary opportunity to be a blog writer. I take keen interest in sports such as football and cricket. I am an avid reader and writer, and also believe that writing blogs will help me realize my true potential. Exchange of information with people has always been something I have loved, as well as the expression of differing views. A few of the core values I believe in are cleanliness, honesty, discipline, and faith.

Assalam o Alaikum, My name Muhammad Usman Qamar and I am from 11 T, I love reading books as well as frequently do poetry. Being a passionate writer, I read informative articles and take interest in sports. My hobbies include playing Table tennis, Football and Cricket whereas I read books between my study breaks. Writing blogs will help me in a better understanding as I am real talkative and am passionate about receiving and conveying messages. This would disclose my true potential where as I would be able to express my views and opinions.

I am Shumahima Zeeshan of 11T. I am a person who is ready to try anything new and challenge my skills.

I have prior experience in blog writing and writing in general as from a young age I have dwelled myself in the world of literature, exploring all different aspects of it. I have written 7 books so far, won several national writing competitions in Saudi Arabia and participated thrice in the Immerse Education Competition, earning a win each time. I can assure you that I am a determined person willing to give my all-in hopes of pleasing you with the content I will present in the nearby future.

Hi there, I am Khizr Shallwani student of grade 10. I am one of the bloggers associated with this platform. Reading books, articles and keeping track of the latest information adds charm to my writing. Being able to share info and my thoughts with you feels like a dream come true. I will be more than happy to keep you updated on things the world is interested in.

Hello everyone, I am Sana Zahid from grade 11T. I am one of the members of the bloggers team. Reading books, newspapers and keeping up with latest trends enhances my writing.  I am so thrilled to be a part of this team as it gives me an opportunity to bring my thoughts and writings in front of the community I cherish.

Hi everyone I’m Rija Salman from 10T, and I’m one of the members of the bloggers team. Reading about any and every kind of animal, and writing adventure filled stories when I get the time, brings me closer and makes me better in the art of creative writing. What I love to do most is share my imagination and ideas through my writings and now I have a chance to display them to a real audience. I hope to please you with my creativity.

Hello readers! I am Hamna from the Jauhar campus, grade 11, and being given the opportunity to express the web of fascinating thoughts in my head takes me over the moon! A bookworm like me could only dream of blogging to put my numberless ideas into words for the audience to read and become a source for their entertainment, even if momentarily it is. May the readers anticipate the best from me and my fellows for our forthcoming works. Fingers crossed, and stay tuned!

Greetings to all!

I’m Zehra of grade 11t and I am pleased to be able to write for our school blog along with my enthusiastic fellows! I’m on cloud nine since ever since I got to know that on this platform I can freely share my notions and overflowing thoughts with you guys every now and then. Other than journaling, I’m a huge fan of baking! It gives me creative freedom, and is a double whammy of awesomeness as you can devour the goodness later as well! Finally, I really hope that through my pieces of writing I keep you all hooked and interested in reading our blog all together with keeping each one of you updated and posted!

Hello everyone, I am Syed Kashan, of grade 11. I am one of the bloggers associated with The City School Jauhar Campus. Reading books, articles and researching about football, nature (includes weather), video-games and cars are my biggest comforts! I look forward to keeping you updated with my take on public matters. I hope to catch your attention with my writing and also keep you informed.

YO, I am Qaiser Khan and I’m from grade 11-T. You can expect lots of astronomy and myth related blogs from me as I’m a fan of both and I hope I can provide you all with interesting information. Out of all the languages, I choose to speak facts so you can rest easy knowing that the information I provide is accurate! I look forward to working as a writer for the blog so stay tuned for the best content from not only me but from all of the other amazing bloggers!

Hello to my fellow readers, I Alishba Riaz from grade 11 am now an honored member of this blog writing team. I am an Art enthusiast, a fashion addict, a fan of reading, music and much more. Expressing creativity and displaying the world from a whole new perspective is my key to giving my writings a skyrocketing boost, I am in seventh heaven to receive an opportunity to portray my words to fuel your hungry minds. Rest assured, my thirst for knowledge will not let you down and keep you up and going. Finally, I sincerely hope that you will have the times of your life with my efforts. So stay tuned and stay positive!

Hey, l’m Fizza Abidi from grade 10; a proud part of the team of bloggers behind this platform. Reading and writing are my biggest comforts which is why I look forward to updating you guys with the latest news as well as sharing my personal take on the matter. I hope to please you guys with my writing while also keeping you informed.
